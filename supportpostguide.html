How to make a good support post!

First off - the title.

You want the title to be a short description of your issue
This could be something like: "pluginName does not show in settings"

Next, a more detailed description of the issue.

Tell us more! Include details such as:

  • Your Operating System (Windows, MacOS, Linux)
  • Anything you tried doing to fix the issue (reload Discord, restart pc)
  • In the case of the example we are using above - does this happen with every plugin?
Useful information like this will help us help you! Try to give as much as possible.

Now, choose an appropriate tag.

Pick tags which relate to your issue. Examples:-
- an issue with installing BetterDiscord would use the Installation tag.
- a question about a plugin would use the `Plugins` and `Question` tags

Finally, post the issue!

Please remember to read the rules, and know that you are not guaranteed help with your issue.
The people doing their best to help are not paid to do so!
