If a tree falls...

Will anyone miss me?
Will anyone remember me?
Will it have any impact on my area?
Will my work replace me immediately?
Will anyone care?

The world will go on.
Earth won't stop spinning
Billions of people will go on with their lives, and not have a single clue.
I'll just be another statistic, along with the other 800 a year.

My heart goes out to all the others whom are suffering.
I hope they find the strength that I cannot.

They say that "life is what you make it," but there is nothing here for me.
I go through the same routines, day in day out; month after month; year after year.

Living is boring.
